01

Automation and Orchestration.

Purevirtual also attaches great importance to automation in its own structures. With this foundation, customers benefit from a precise, reliable and cost-effective way of working at all levels - from data center to client administration. This is made possible by the in-house management platform confluenta core.

02

Deployment.

The deployment of virtual servers and physical clients is automated, as is the subsequent functional testing. In this way purevirtual GmbH guarantees an integrated quality assurance, which prevents later efforts by working cleanly from the beginning.

03

Management.

All systems are managed by the central platform confluenta core. This management approach enables the team to work pro-actively and at the same time leads to a high stability of all systems.

04

Monitoring.

The continuous monitoring connected to the central platform creates the ideal basis for self-healing systems. If the solution reports more complex problems, the purevirtual GmbH team can react immediately and eliminate impending difficulties before problems arise in operation.

05

Patching.

To ensure the highest possible security, all infrastructure components of purevirtual GmbH are centrally patched. Through close cooperation with manufacturers, the team can react quickly if necessary. Furthermore, the internal quality assurance enables roll-backs when necessary. Thanks to the confluenta core platform, operational stability is guaranteed at all times.

06

Virtualization.

Virtualization is one of the core competencies of purevirtual GmbH. We rely on established host hardware from Dell and on a reliable network backbone with Juniper and Cisco components. A sophisticated out-of-band management on hardware level manages and monitors all machines in 24x7 mode. At the same time, we guarantee consistently high quality in the setup of the hypervisors.

In order to meet the most diverse customer requirements, purevirtual GmbH operates highly available and multi-site clusters based on KVM, VMware and Citrix Hypervisor. In addition, the team's technicians are appropriately certified in handling the third-party solutions.

07

Server.

As a basic service, purevirtual GmbH offers the virtualization of all relevant server applications - of course with high availability and across locations. The operation is equally possible via Windows and Linux systems. Intensively thought-out fail-safe concepts guarantee an availability of over 99.9 percent.

08

Clients / VDI (Virtual Desktop Infrastructure).

Virtualized user workspaces enable mobile working and make the performance of the end device obsolete. Companies can obtain the necessary hardware via confluenta scale without any capital expenditure and thus benefit from proven Citrix technology. By means of VDI, purevirtual GmbH enables customers to access their familiar desktops from any location and on any device. The confluenta core platform also offers automated processes and an established connection protocol.

09

Applications.

In addition to clients, purevirtual GmbH also offers fully virtualized applications that are loaded into the existing operating system. With this service, customers also receive Windows-only applications for Apple OSX devices, for example. With this support, software manufacturers can also offer their solutions via the confluenta core as a service platform.

10

3D / CAD.

The purevirtual GmbH team has many years of intensive experience with the virtualization of graphics-intensive workloads, especially in the CAD environment. Engineers and architects benefit from the active cooperation between purevirtual GmbH and Citrix and Nvidia. Thanks to this partnership, the team is always up-to-date when it comes to technological innovations or the optimization of networks for 3D loads. Via confluenta scale customers have the possibility to purchase 3D solutions on a rental basis.

11

Mobile working.

Mobile workspaces and flexible home office solutions are becoming increasingly important with digitalization. purevirtual GmbH offers a solid, stable technology base, on the basis of which working via LTE as well as SIM cards integrated in the notebook is very easy. Companies can obtain the necessary hardware via confluenta scale without investment costs.

12

Site networking.

Reliable site networking is one of the core challenges of the coming years. Therefore, the team of purevirtual GmbH works in this area in a requirement-oriented manner and develops the best concept for each individual customer. Line optimization and redundancies are part of the self-evident standard.

13

SD-WAN.

Using SD-WAN, redundant site networking is achieved on the one hand and the optimization of lines and the connection to the public cloud (Azure, AWS, Google Cloud platform) on the other. Especially the stable, resilient and fast cloud connection is becoming more and more relevant in digitization. With purevirtual GmbH, companies can already prepare themselves for the future today. The provision and management of the SD-WAN solution is based on the confluenta core platform and can be obtained flexibly and scalably via confluenta scale.

14

Ethernet.

Some business cases require very broadband and latency optimized solutions. Thanks to many years of experience and resilient contacts to highly specialized carriers, purevirtual GmbH can provide optimal support in such cases. The team designs, procures and supports the necessary Layer-2 solutions, adapted to individual requirements - optionally also based on dark fibre solutions.

15

E-Mail.

E-mail is still the essential means of communication within and between companies. Accordingly, it is important that this medium can be used at all times to keep daily business moving.

16

Hosted Exchange / Office 365.

Customers of purevirtual GmbH can choose to use hosted Exchange servers from the highly secure data centers in Frankfurt or get a direct connection to Office 365 through Cloud Connect. As a Microsoft partner, the purevirtual GmbH team has the best possible relationships here and also enables the use of Office 365 solutions via confluenta scale.

17

Mail Archiving.

An audit-proof mail archive should be mandatory for every company. With confluenta scale, purevirtual GmbH offers its customers the opportunity to obtain such a legally compliant archive in highly secure data centers with multiple backups and end-to-end encryption on a monthly basis. In addition, such an archive can be stored fully encrypted in the customer company at any time.

18

Mail Encryption.

Due to the increase in phishing attacks, the digital signature as well as the actual encryption of mails is becoming more and more important. In principle, the authenticity of the sender should always be verifiable by means of a digital signature and confidential content should be encrypted - also against the background of the EU-DSGVO. With confluenta scale, purevirtual GmbH offers a secure, practical and directly usable solution on a rental basis.

19

Telephony.

Telephony continues to be a very important part of all companies' operations. Therefore, purevirtual GmbH in cooperation with Starface offers a reliable telephony solutionTelephony solution that meets all requirements of a modern telephone system. Together, the focus is on mobile working and companies can also use these solutions on a rental basis with confluenta scale.
