Infrastructure on-premise.

Companies that need to keep their data (or part of it) on site at their location for internal reasons or due to industry-specific requirements benefit from the managed on-premise offering of purevirtual GmbH.

Without their own IT department or suitably trained employees and without large investments in hardware, companies can use managed servers directly at their location. After careful selection together with the company's managers, our team puts together the optimal hardware, installs it on site and takes care of configuration, maintenance, troubleshooting and all other infrastructure issues without having to travel to the site every time.

This is achieved by means of a remote connection, independent of electricity and the Internet, via an interface specially developed for this purpose. With this service, companies can fully rely on their stable infrastructure in daily business and have the flexibility to have hardware components adapted to changing requirements at any time without having to invest in acquisition costs themselves.
