The IT infrastructure is the blood circulation of every company.

The management has to concentrate on its core tasks and does not have the capacities and the necessary expertise to take care of this important foundation itself. This makes it all the more important to have a professional partner who can act as an independent advisor to support decisions and thus ensure that the infrastructure always provides a solid basis for current business and future strategies.

Consulting and operations are not mutually exclusive, but harmonize ideally - after all, digitization is always interdisciplinary in its principles. At Purevirtual, in addition to high-quality, open-ended consulting, you also have the option of having the optimal infrastructure you have chosen implemented and managed directly by competent experts - all from a single source, without tedious coordination loops across several specialist teams.

Whether pure consulting services, support as external project manager, purchasing support or simultaneous operation of running structures - companies get all services from one source.

The Purevirtual process of work.

01

Strategic planning of the future infrastructure.

Your business is constantly evolving and your infrastructure must be able to cope with this development at all times. Just as the requirements for performance, speed, efficiency and data volume change, the infrastructure must also be adapted to provide you with a solid foundation.

02

Decision support for external offers.

The market is full of software and application manufacturers who offer their different solutions and systems at individual conditions. Due to the multitude of applications, products and selection criteria, it is usually difficult for management to evaluate such offers objectively and make the right decision for their own company.

03

Professional risk analysis.

Many companies lack a transparent insight into possible default risks. However, it is important to know your own points of attack and potential weaknesses in order to be able to secure them accordingly. Incompatibilities in heterogeneous ecosystems, a lack of transparency across all systems, failures due to capacity bottlenecks, outdated technologies.

04

Future-oriented potential analysis.

Looking into the future is not always easy. Nevertheless, it is crucial for companies to recognize and use their own potential in order to stay ahead of the competition. For example, a more powerful infrastructure with more computing power and better connectivity can form the basis for real-time data analysis that organizations can use to optimize their production processes.
