Infrastructure as a Service.

With the IaaS offering from purevirtual GmbH you benefit from our partnerships with the reliable cloud providers Microsoft and Citrix. Or optionally use a state-of-the-art, secure and scalable infrastructure that is specially designed for the fail-safe, efficient and sustainable operation of business-critical applications and systems. On 9,600 m2 of newly built data center space, the FRA15 data center impresses with 19,000 kW of power capacity and direct connection to the DE-CIX Internet exchange node.

Specially trained security and technical personnel guard the facility around the clock and state-of- the-art early fire detection and fire fighting systems ensure the highest possible security. The shared use of this infrastructure is a cost-efficient way to benefit from hardware for which hardly any company can afford the necessary budget in this form. For particularly sensitive data, there is also the option of renting dedicated racks on which only the data of your own company is stored and to which no third party can physically gain access.

Thanks to the point-to-point connection from FRA15 directly to the respective company, connectivity is guaranteed fail-safe at all times. purevirtual GmbH takes care that the connection is guaranteed at all times - among other things via WAN / Ethernet Connect on a fiber optic basis with additional redundancies via various Internet connections such as cable, DSL, LTE and many more - naturally also via VPN. For smaller locations and branch offices where no conventional WAN is possible, software-defined WAN solutions are also available. This means that there are no restrictions whatsoever on data storage outside the company walls - on the contrary: management benefits from certified security concepts and the teams always work as if they were sitting directly in the data center.
